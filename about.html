ABOUT US

Company Overview

The C.E.O of Madoms Global Trade, Mr Segun Durojaye, is a seasoned entrepreneur and holds numerous certifications in various fields such as Logistics, Clearing and Forwarding, Project Management, and Financing. He has provided leadership for numerous companies in finance management, import and export trade processes, project management, and trade-finance consultation services.

Madoms Global Trade was established in 2014 as a financing company and has now emerged as one of the leading names in the Trade Finance Industry. Madoms Global Trade is a platform that serves you with the best expertise to understand your complex trade transactions and ensure you get timely and optimal solutions for your business requirements. Our comprehensive range of trade finance solutions allows our clients to gain access to cross-border opportunities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to use expertise, technology and networks to become the centre of excellence in financial solutions and also provide our cliens with what they need to push their transactions with competitive charges.
